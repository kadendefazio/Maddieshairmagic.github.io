About Me

Let me formally introduce myself! I've had a passion for all things beauty and art since I was very young. Being a theater kid growing up, I had a lot of oppurtunities to learn about makeup and hair, which eventually made me fall in love with it. My freshman year of highschool (2019) I started Parkway West CTC's cosmotology program. During those years, I've developed all my basic knowledge and then some for the beauty industry. I completed all 4 years there and graduated in May of 2023. In July of that year, I went for the state boards and passed, which resulted in me getting my cosmotology license. Over the years, I found what I am best at, particularly hair, but I've also grown a likeness for more fun/transformative cuts and vivid hair. Anything extreme and creative is right up my alley. I even experiment with these different hair visions I have on myself! I currently am an apprentice at Finch Beauty and Bridal.
